FAQ Desk

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions. You can also chat with our live planner widget at the bottom right.

How do I reserve a tour package?
You can reserve a spot by clicking the 'Book Now' button in the navbar or on any package card. Simply fill out the contact form with your desired tour, travel date, and group size. A reference number will be generated immediately.
What is included in the package prices?
Our tour package prices generally include local hotel/chalet accommodation, ground transit during the tour, breakfast daily, entry tickets for destinations, and a certified local guide. Flights are not included.
Do you provide custom travel itineraries?
Yes! We design custom tours for families, honey-mooner couples, and corporate groups. Simply send us an inquiry specifying your desired destinations and budget using our contact form.
